*([सायणो [ऽत्र](https://archive.org/details/SamaVedaSanhitaWithSayanabhashyaVolume1SatyavrataSamasrami1874bis/page/n581&sa=D&ust=1542425956431000) कौथुमभाष्ये। जमदग्निः। मित्रः, अर्यमा, वरुणः।])*
