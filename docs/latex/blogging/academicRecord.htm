Advice obtained through Chinmayi.

The following are Chinmayi's impressions about working with Prof. Dhillon, based on conversations with Prateek Jain, who is a very successful student graduating in December and starting work in MSR India.

It is not a good idea to trust Prof. Dhillon for ideas and project guidance. You must take initiative and be independent. He may not be very good at supplying problems or deriving solutions actively. Even if he shoots it down, you should not leave a promising idea, but fully develop it and turn it into a research paper on your own initiative; then he will be happy and support publication. Prateek, through contact with the highly independent Suvrit Sra, learnt to be independent.
