Tour plan

Places to visit:

In Flagstaff: Sunset Volcanic crater; Meteor crater; Lowell observatory. Snowbowl.

Grand canyon.

The schedule of activities:

Friday:

Go to Enterprise at 1615. Rent a car. Refresh the memory of driving on normal roads, and on highways till 2000. Buy food supply for stay in Tempe and for the tour. Consider visiting Tempe lake, and kayaking.

Saturday:

Set out an hour before sunrise, at 0630. Drive to the Sunset volcanic crater, Flagstaff. Eat breakfast at Flagstaff for .5 hours. 3 hours drive. 1000.

Spend 1 hours at the volcanic crater.

Drive up to the South Rim of the Grand Canyon. 3.75 hours drive. .5 hours breakfast break.

Reach the south rim of the canyon by 1130. View sights at the Grand canyon for a couple of hours.

Drive up to Horseshoe bend. Watch the sight for half an hour.

Camp in Marble Canyon or in Zion national park.
